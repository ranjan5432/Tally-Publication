About Us

We are India’s leading tally books publishing company providing the most comprehensive self-learning tally book for complete tally solutions. Our tally GST book guides and helps you in managing the business and technology complexities of today’s economy. You will learn the concepts of GST and related transactions for supply of goods and services, exports, imports, advanced entries pertaining to composition and unregistered dealers, SEZ and exempted supplies.

We are associated with Tally Education for many years and earned a good reputation in providing in-depth understanding of financial accounting and GST features along with hands on experience on Tally, India’s most preferred business management software. Our tally book is enriched with numerous screen shots, solved illustrations as well as practice scenarios to help the students in understanding the intricacies of business operations and enable them to handle such transactions in the daily work environment efficiently. Our tally GST book can serve both the students and the teaching community.
